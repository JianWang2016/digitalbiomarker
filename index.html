What is Digital Biomarker


Digital biomarkers are participant-generated physiological or behavioral measures collected through digital devices in either controlled environments or free living conditions.


Digital biomarkers represent an opportunity to more frequently capture clinically meaningful and objective data in cost-effective and patient-friendly manner. When correlations are established to gold standard metrics, digital biomarkers can be used to explain or predicted health-related outcomes.
